    Information about Anchorage:

    The City of Anchorage was settled in 1914, but was incorporated on November 23, 1920. According to the US Census Anchorage has a Population of 287,145 People. Also, according to the US census, the average income in Anchorage is 88,871 per household while the rest of Alaska is at $80,287. Lastly, Anchorage is an Urban area and it is Southcentral region.
